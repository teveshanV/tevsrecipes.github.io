Greek Ribs

Looking for a flavorful and exotic way to jazz up your dinner tonight? Look no further than these Greek Ribs! Packed with aromatic herbs, tangy lemon juice, and sweet honey, these succulent ribs are sure to transport your taste buds straight to the Mediterranean. So, roll up your sleeves, tie on your apron, and get ready to take your taste buds on a delicious adventure. Opa!

prep time

20 min.

cook time

2 hrs.

serving

4 to 6 servings

instructions

step 1

Preheat oven to 175°C. Line a baking sheet with foil and set aside.

step 2

In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, oregano, rosemary, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper.

step 3

Rub the mixture all over the ribs, making sure to cover all sides.

step 4

Place the ribs on the prepared baking sheet and bake for 1 hour.

step 5

In a separate bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, chicken broth, and honey.

step 6

After the first hour of baking, brush the ribs with the lemon-honey mixture.

step 7

Return the ribs to the oven and bake for an additional hour, basting with the lemon-honey mixture every 20 minutes.

step 8

Remove the ribs from the oven and let them rest for 5 minutes before slicing.

step 9

Serve with lemon wedges and fresh parsley for garnish. Enjoy your delicious Greek Ribs!

ingredients

2 kgs pork spare ribs

2 tbsp olive oil

2 tbsp dried oregano

1 tbsp dried rosemary

1tbsp garlic powder

1 tsp salt

1 tsp black pepper

½ cup fresh lemon juice

½ cup chicken broth

¼ cup honey

Lemon wedges and fresh parsley, for garnish

tools

Baking Sheet

Foil

Small Bowl

Whisk

Brush for basting
