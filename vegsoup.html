Veg Soup

There's something truly magical about a warm bowl of vegetable soup on a chilly day. It's like a big hug in a bowl, filled with colorful vegetables and bursting with flavor. So if you're in need of some warmth and comfort, this vegetable soup recipe is just what the doctor ordered!

prep time

20 min.

cook time

45 min

serving

6 to 8 servings

instructions

step 1

In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and saut√© until the onion is translucent and the garlic is fragrant, about 3-4 minutes.

step 2

Add the carrots, celery, and potatoes to the pot. Cook for another 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.

step 3

Pour in the diced tomatoes and vegetable broth, and add the thyme, rosemary, and bay leaf. Bring the soup to a simmer.

step 4

Reduce the heat to low and let the soup simmer for 30-35 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender and the flavors have melded together.

step 5

Remove the bay leaf and discard. Season the soup with salt and pepper, to taste.

step 6

Stir in the chopped spinach and let it wilt in the soup for 2-3 minutes.

step 7

Serve hot and enjoy!

ingredients

2 tablespoons olive oil

1 onion, chopped

3 garlic cloves, minced

3 carrots, chopped

3 celery stalks, chopped

2 medium potatoes, peeled and diced

1 can diced tomatoes (500 g)

1 teaspoon dried thyme

1 teaspoon dried rosemary

1 bay leaf

Salt and pepper, to taste

2 cups chopped fresh spinach

tools

Large pot

Cutting board

Chef's knife

Wooden spoon
